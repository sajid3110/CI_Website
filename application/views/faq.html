Faq's

1. How much do your wedding films cost?

Most of the budgeting depend upon the size and type of the wedding. Each wedding film is customisable as per your requirements. We take into consideration a lot of factors including your story, personality and how much of the wedding you want to remember. The cost also depends upon the size of crew and number of days of the wedding. Added services such as a personalised soundtrack, aerial videography, on-site edits and any other special requirements would be an additional cost. Having said that, each wedding is unique and your films could cost a little less, or much more. Our approach to film-making and budgeting isn't quite as cut dry as a 'package' or 'fixed price'. So whether you're having a Bollywood style grand production or an intimate backyard wedding, we'd like to treat it with the poignancy and grandeur it deserves. Write to us and we'll do our best to make it work for you.

2. Do you offer same day edits?

We do offer same-day edits, but it is not something we recommend because a hurried edit may not have the same amount of heart and passion in the film compared to a complete post production process. Besides, we have been told that when it comes to our films, it is worth the wait.

3. How big is your crew?

The crew we provide is tailored to the requirements of your wedding. We can send anywhere from a 5 people crew to a 12 people crew. We assure you that you can trust our instinct and we will produce a perfect wedding film for you

4. Do you do wedding photography as well?

Yes we do wedding photography as well.

5. How long are your wedding films

The wedding films we make depend on the size or type of wedding. With each cinematic video package we provide a 30 to 60 minute highlight video shot in documentary style with an apt storyline. We also provide a 3 to 5 minute wedding trailer with the best moments of the wedding also shot in documentary style with an apt storyline with interviews with friends and family.

6. How soon can i get my wedding film?

It takes us between 6 to 8 months to edit and give final touches to your wedding trailer and wedding film. But once again, we have been told that it is worth the wait.

7. How soon can i get my wedding photographs?

You can get your wedding photographs in three months after you have given us the selection of images you want edited from the raw images.

8. What services can you offer other than wedding films and photographs?

Other than wedding films and photographs, we provide our services for birthdays, events, product shoots, ad film production, corporate videos, property shoots, 360 degree photography and VFX animation.

9. Do you guys travel?

Yes we do travel. But you would have to arrange for our travel and our stay

10. Are the prices negotiable?

Making wedding films is an expensive affair that takes a lot of equipments and man-hours to create perfect memories for you. Having said that, we do try our best to fit you in our pricing packages because we want to be part of your special day.
