About Us

We are storytellers. If we had to put a tag to ourselves we would say we are a video production house. But what we really do is play with the visual medium to tell interesting stories across genres - be it capturing uncontrived moments on ‘the’ big day of your life, a corporate ad that needs to connect with your audience, or celebrate the arrival of your little one - we use the simple craft of storytelling to put words and frames to your emotions. We have worked primarily with a diverse set of verticals such as Real Estate, Automobile, Hospitality, Tourism, E-commerce, Fashion, and more.

Simply put, we do what we do because that is what makes our soul jump with joy. We strive toward evoking a similar emotion from those who work with us.

The Founders

Rahul Soman

After honing his photography skills under the mentorship of internationally acclaimed fashion photographer, Harmut Norenberg, Rahul knew exactly what his life’s calling was. After grabbing eyeballs with his tastefully edited videos, he went on to learn the intricacies and skills required for photographing infants and children from acclaimed Australian photographer, Sandra el Ayobi. Rahul spearheads just about everything at Jumping Souls, whilst scaling it to new heights, one frame at a time.

Krishna Devate

A self-taught photographer, Krishna possesses a degree in Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering, and has worked with MNCs such as Infosys, in the past. However, the burning desire to pursue professionally his deep rooted passion for capturing moving and still images resulted in a leap of faith that has been one of Krishna’s best decisions yet. At Jumping Souls, Krishna plays every card right - from running the show, to capturing breathtaking images, and editing videos.

What we do

Wedding's

We love weddings. The explosion of colour, smiles, emotions and warmth – all play a key role in the narrative that we weave to tell your story. More importantly, we love the fact that we get to be involved in making your big day special. We integrate the documentary style of filmmaking into our wedding films, well, because every union of two beautiful souls has a different story to tell.

Newborn and maternal photography

They say, ‘With the arrival of the little one, the mother is also born’. We understand how beautiful and personal this phase is for you. We capture and document the entire process of motherhood – right from the pregnancy phase to the birth – and compile it to create a timeline of this beautiful progression. We also capture the innocent expressions of your newborn in a creative yet safe setting, to freeze these precious and irretrievable moments for posterity.

Corporate

For every business, it is important to communicate to its audience in the right way and convey the right message. We acknowledge that and help corporate agencies in achieving this with our creative ad campaigns and films. Be it a showroom walkthrough, a property shoot, the launch of the product of your labour or capturing corporate events such as conferences, team building activities, workshops, trade shows and concerts - we do it all.

For the tech geeks: We use state-of-the-art gear from Sony for all our shoots. We shoot in S-LOG which provides us the flexibility in colour grading, using DaVinci resolve, to lend the desired cinematic look and feel to the output.
